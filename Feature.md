
**Program Features**

**Cloud-Based System**
The veterinary clinic system operates on the cloud, allowing access anytime, anywhere, making management more convenient.

**Unlimited Data Storage**
The clinic and animal hospital system allows unlimited storage of customer and patient data, and supports ID card readers with automatic integration.

**Works on All Devices**
Designed for clinics, animal hospitals, and pet hotels, the system supports all devices with a responsive and user-friendly design.

**Allergy Recording**
Records drug allergies and underlying conditions of animal patients to ensure accurate and safe prescriptions.

**Service Queue Management**
Supports queue management for services, reducing congestion and improving operational efficiency.

**Product Transfer**
Allows instant transfer of products and medications within the hospital or between branches, ideal for multi-location clinics and pet hotels.

**Treatment Records**
Veterinarians can record treatments using a Problem Solving Pattern for accuracy and standardized care.

**Hospitalization Management**
Daily inpatient care can be recorded using time slots for systematic and continuous care in clinics, hospitals, and pet hotels.

**Multiple Payment Methods**
Supports various payment methods including cash, credit cards, and transfers for convenient transactions.

**X-ray Recording**
Stores X-ray results and images for complete historical tracking within the system.

**DICOM Image Support**
Displays and stores X-ray and ultrasound images in patient records for systematic follow-up.

**Treatment Image Recording**
Capture and attach treatment images directly from mobile devices to patient records.

**Lab Results Recording**
Records lab results and values for accurate and continuous treatment monitoring.

**PDF Document Storage**
Stores important documents such as lab results and treatment reports securely and allows easy retrieval.

**Drawing for Treatment Planning**
Enhances treatment accuracy by allowing visual annotations and treatment planning within the system.

**Statistical Data**
Provides snapshot reports of status and performance to support fast and accurate planning.

**Retail System**
Supports retail sales in pet shops with barcode scanning and product search for convenience.

**Grooming Queue Management**
Manages grooming queues with booking and appointment tracking for efficient service.

**Inventory Management System**
Supports purchasing, receiving, stock counting, and stock movement tracking with a complete stock card system.

**Appointment Scheduling**
Allows advance booking for patients and checks doctor schedules to prevent overlaps.

**SMS and LINE Notifications**
Sends appointment reminders via SMS and LINE OA for better queue management.

**Loyalty Points System**
Enables point accumulation and redemption for discounts, adding value for customers.

**Membership System**
Defines membership types and discounts by group or specific items for flexible benefit management.

**Discount System**
Allows pre-setting various discount formats for quick and easy application.

**Pet and Owner Reminders**
Sets reminders for pets and owners to ensure continuous care and follow-ups.

**Blood Bank Management**
Records blood donations and transfusions for systematic tracking and treatment support.

**Vaccination Records**
Tracks pet vaccination history accurately and conveniently.

**Doctor Scheduling**
Allows advance scheduling of doctors to improve appointment and treatment management.

**Hospital Operating Hours**
Sets opening and closing times for each branch to improve appointment planning.

**Hospital Information Setup**
Configures address and contact details for each branch for easier communication.

**User Activity Monitoring**
Admins can track user login history to enhance security and control.

**Access Control Management**
Defines user permissions by group to ensure proper data access control.

**Login Time Restrictions**
Allows setting individual access time windows to prevent inappropriate system usage.
